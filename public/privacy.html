Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

Focus Valley ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

Data stored locally on your device:

  • Timer settings and preferences
  • Focus session history and statistics
  • Garden collection and plant data
  • To-do list items
  • Category configurations
  • Sound mixer preferences

This data is stored in your browser's localStorage. If you sign in, it is also synced to our cloud (Supabase) for cross-device access.

Account information (optional):

  • If you create an account, we collect your email address via email/password or Google sign-in (Supabase Auth)
  • Your account enables cloud sync across devices
  • You can delete your account and associated data at any time

Analytics data:

  • We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how the app is used
  • This includes anonymous usage patterns such as feature usage, session duration, and device type
  • Google Analytics may use cookies to distinguish users
  • No personally identifiable information is sent to Google Analytics

Error monitoring:

  • We use Sentry to detect and diagnose application errors
  • Error reports may include browser type, OS, and stack traces — no personal data

Location data:

  • With your permission, the app may request your approximate location to fetch local weather data from Open-Meteo
  • Location data is sent directly to Open-Meteo and is not stored on our servers

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Focus Valley experience
  • To understand usage patterns and prioritize features
  • To enable cloud sync for signed-in users
  • To diagnose technical issues

3. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — Authentication and cloud data sync. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics — Usage analytics. Google Privacy Policy
  • Sentry — Error monitoring. Sentry Privacy Policy
  • Open-Meteo — Weather data. Open-Meteo Terms
  • Vercel — Hosting and delivery. Vercel Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — Font delivery (Sora typeface)

4. Data Retention

Local data persists until you clear your browser storage. Cloud-synced data is retained as long as your account is active; you may request deletion at any time. Analytics data is retained per Google Analytics' default retention settings (14 months).

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your local data (it's already on your device)
  • Export your data via the CSV export feature
  • Delete your local data by clearing browser storage
  • Request deletion of your cloud account and all associated data
  • Opt out of analytics by using browser extensions like Google Analytics Opt-out

6. Children's Privacy

Focus Valley is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Questions? Contact us at privacy@focusvalley.app
